Behind the code

Hi, I’m Jean: a recipient of the NCWIT Digital Initiatives scholarship, who will be graduating Grand Circus’ C# .NET Fulltime Bootcamp in December 2020.

For most, 2020 has been a tumultuous year. Despite the obstacles this year has presented, I’ve also seen a lot of opportunity for personal growth. It’s given me the space to reflect my career goals, and ultimately led me the decision to pursue a more fulfilling career as a developer. Technology has always been a passion of mine. Being raised from a time when the absence of technology led to the abundance of it, I was naturally curious and wanted to explore it.

One of my earliest memories was learning how to use a computer and my first experiments involved making my own webpage, which involved plugging some code into a GeoCities website and seeing the immediate result. It was rudimentary HTML, anime pictures taken from a google search and a generous dose of pixelated sparkles. An eyesore by today’s standards, but back then, I was proud of making it.

Now, as an adult, I’m driven by this lifelong passion and I’m working toward a career as a full-stack developer. Although my background is tied strongly to social media and the automotive industry, my expertise around customer experience, relationship building, and staying on top of social/cultural trends are invaluable skills that will carry over to the next chapter of my career.

While I am currently attending bootcamp, I am teaching myself HTML/CSS and JavaScript in my downtime by developing an online portfolio website to showcase my work. All updates can be found here. Also, feel free to reach out using any of the links that I've provided above.
